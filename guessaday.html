Guessaday

A trainer for calculating the day of the week of any given date

This is a free Android App which displays a date and asks you what day of the week that date is. Then it checks your answer. Optionally it measures how long it took you.

on froid: Guessaday
on github: source
on Google-Play: Guessaday

also available as app in the jolla store.
